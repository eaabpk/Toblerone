Our history



The beloved chocolate was created in 1908 in the Swiss capital of Bern by Theodor Tobler and his cousin Emil Baumann. One year later, Toblerone became the first patented milk chocolate.

The white bear hiding in the logo is symbol of the birthplace of the confectionary.


The town is near the Matterhorn mountain - which inspired the unique triangular shape of the unusual bar.


Theodor’s sons have suggested that the triangular shape actually originates from a pyramid shape that dancers at the Folies Bergères - a French cabaret hall - created in a show.


More than 100 years later Toblerone is still famous and favourite to many generations.
