Toblerone Ice-cream


10 - 12 people



Prep. 15 min.
Cook. 6h



Method:Refrigerate/ Freeze

INGREDIENTS


  • 200g Toblerone chocolate
  • 4 Egg yolks
  • 1/2 c + 1 tablespoon powdered sugar
  • 2 c Heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Fresh raspberries for decoration



STEPS



Melt the chocolate



Beat eggs and sugar good then add the vanilla



Whip the heavy cream



Pour the chocolate into the egg and sugar mix. Gently turn the wipped cream into the chocolate mixture. Be sure you reach what is in the bottom of the bowl and gather it up all. Pour the final mixture into a glass container and freeze for at least 6 hours. Serve with fresh fruits like strawberries or raspberries.
